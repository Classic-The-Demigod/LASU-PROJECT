Lagos State University - also known as LASU - was established in 1983 for the advancement of learning and establishment of academic excellence. The university caters for a population of over 35,000 students enrolled full-time and offers diploma, degree and post graduate programmes including a MBA programme. The University is located in Ojo, a town in Lagos State, Nigeria, West Africa
